Understanding Package Management

Our Package Management system is designed to streamline the process of handling deliveries for residents and management alike. It provides a centralized log for all incoming and outgoing packages, ensuring nothing gets lost or misplaced.

Key features include:

  • Automated notifications sent to residents upon package arrival.
  • Digital signature capture for secure package pickups.
  • Real-time tracking of package status from delivery to pickup.

Amenity Reservation Guide

The Amenity Reservation system allows residents to conveniently book common facilities online, reducing conflicts and improving accessibility. This includes spaces like gyms, party rooms, guest suites, and more.

How it works:

  • View real-time availability of all bookable amenities.
  • Securely reserve time slots and manage your bookings.
  • Receive instant confirmations and reminders for your reservations.

Visitor Management & Security

Our Visitor Management system enhances building security by allowing residents to pre-register their guests. This streamlines the entry process for visitors while maintaining a comprehensive log for security purposes.

Benefits include:

  • Faster and more secure check-ins for pre-registered guests.
  • Digital records of all visitor entries and exits.
  • Improved overall building security and resident peace of mind.
